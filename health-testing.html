What These Tests Mean

Hip & Elbow Dysplasia

Hip and elbow dysplasia are degenerative joint diseases that can cause pain, lameness, and arthritis. Buddy's normal ratings indicate proper joint structure and reduced risk of passing dysplasia to offspring.

Eye Diseases

Collie Eye Anomaly (CEA) and Progressive Retinal Atrophy (PRA) can lead to vision loss. Buddy's clear status ensures healthy offspring with excellent ocular health.

Genetic Markers

DNA testing confirms Buddy carries no known harmful genetic mutations common in the breed, supporting genetic diversity and health in future generations.

Responsible Breeding

These comprehensive tests reflect our commitment to ethical breeding practices. We believe that responsible breeders must health test to:

  • Prevent hereditary diseases
  • Improve breed health overall
  • Protect puppy buyers from expensive health issues
  • Support the long-term viability of the breed
